How to select service

how to upload documents

Capture clear photos to your documents then attach them to App:

  • 1 - NATIONAL ID ( UPLOAD TWO PHOTOS )
  • 2 - DRIVING LICENSE ( UPLOAD TWO PHOTOS )
  • 3 - CAR LICENSE ( UPLOAD TWO PHOTOS )

Account Confirmation

After uploading your Documents

  • you will receive a call to arrange tests appointment.
  • Then our team will review your documents.
  • and your account will be ready very soon.
  • After confirming your account, you will be able to start receive clients requests.
  • In case of having something wrong while you sign up, Please Contact Support Team.

Going online/offline

  • Go to Menu > Online/Offline, to control receiving trips.

Receive requests

  • When you receive a new request. You will receive a notification sound and a popup.
  • and a fully detailed ticket about new trip
  • ( contains client contact - Pickup & destination - Truck details & photos - trip time )

Navigation

  • Use google maps services in WINCH app, OR check destination details manually on map through zooming in.

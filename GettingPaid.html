How to see my earnings

Go on Menu > Earnings

  • 1 - Check your current Balance
  • 2 - Checkout history
  • 3 - Earnings details

How payment is calculated.

  • Base fare = 100 LE
  • Minimum charge per trip = 240 LE
  • The price per K.M = 4 LE
  • Price per minute = 1.75 LE

Trip calculation example

  • Trip start point at Al Serag Mall - Nasr City.
  • Trip end point at Sharbatli Mosque - 5th assembly.
    • Distance : 17 Km.
    • Duration : 40 minutes.
  • The trip bill is calculated as following :
    • Cost of open trip : 100 LE
    • Cost of distance calculation : 17 * 4 = 68 LE
    • Trip duration : 40 * 1.75 = 70 LE
    • Total trip cost : 238 LE
